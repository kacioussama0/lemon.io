Lemon Benifits

Lemons contain a high amount of vitamin C, fiber, and plant compounds that give them a number of health benefits. Lemons may aid weight loss and reduce your risk of heart disease, anemia, kidney stones, digestive issues,and cancer

Did you know you can use the leaves from a lemon tree to make a isingly delicious tea? It has a citrus note from the lemons and is packed full of antioxidants that are good for you.
